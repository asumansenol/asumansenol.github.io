Expert Privacy & Data Solutions

Our team combines academic research excellence with hands-on industry experience. Led by a Ph.D. in Web Privacy and backed by 8+ years of Fortune 500 data engineering expertise, we deliver solutions that are both technically sound and business-focused.

Privacy Engineering: Building Trust Through Technical Excellence

Privacy isn't just about compliance—it's about building trust with your users and creating sustainable business practices. Our privacy engineering services help you integrate privacy considerations into every aspect of your technology stack, from system architecture to user interfaces.

  • Privacy-by-Design architecture and implementation
  • GDPR, CCPA, and international privacy regulation compliance
  • Data Subject Rights (DSR) automation systems
  • Privacy impact assessments and risk mitigation
  • Secure data processing and anonymization techniques

Our approach is grounded in both academic research and practical implementation. With publications at top-tier conferences (WWW, IEEE S&P, USENIX) and the 2022 CNIL-Inria Award for Privacy Protection, we bring cutting-edge knowledge to real-world challenges.

Whether you're building a new product or retrofitting privacy into existing systems, we design solutions that protect user data while enabling your business to thrive. Our experience spans web applications, mobile platforms, and enterprise systems across industries from fintech to healthcare.
